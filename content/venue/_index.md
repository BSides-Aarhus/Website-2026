BSides Aarhus 2026 takes place at **INCUBA Next** in the Katrinebjerg area of Aarhus — the city's technology and innovation hub.

## Getting There

**By public transport:** Take bus 2A or 11 to Katrinebjerg. The venue is a short walk from the bus stop.

**By car:** There is parking available in the surrounding area. Follow signs to Katrinebjerg / IT-byen.

**By train:** Aarhus Central Station is approximately 3 km from the venue. Take bus 2A from the station directly to Katrinebjerg.

## About the Venue

INCUBA Next is a modern innovation hub located in the heart of Katrinebjerg, Aarhus's technology district. The building offers excellent facilities for conferences, including multiple presentation rooms, networking areas, and catering facilities.
