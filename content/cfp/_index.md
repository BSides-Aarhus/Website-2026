We're looking for speakers! BSides Aarhus 2026 welcomes talks on all aspects of information security. Whether you're a seasoned speaker or presenting for the first time, we want to hear from you.

## Important Dates

- **Call for Presentations Closes:** Friday, April 10, 2026
- **Speaker Notification:** Monday, April 20, 2026
- **Speaker Confirmation Due:** Monday, April 27, 2026
- **Schedule Published:** Friday, May 1, 2026
- **Conference:** Saturday, June 20, 2026

## Topics of Interest

- **Offensive Security** — exploitation, red teaming, penetration testing
- **Defensive Security** — blue team tactics, threat hunting, forensics, incident response
- **AI & Emerging Technologies** — generative AI, LLMs, cryptography
- **Engineering & Code** — secure coding, reverse engineering, malware analysis
- **Infrastructure** — cloud security, virtualization, mobile security
- **Network & Hardware** — wireless, telecommunications, embedded devices
- **Human Elements** — social engineering, privacy, GRC
- **Live Demonstrations** — tool demos, proof-of-concept walkthroughs

## Talk Format

- **Duration:** 45 minutes (40 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A)
- **Tracks:** Two parallel tracks
- **Delivery:** In-person only at INCUBA Next, Aarhus
- **Language:** English or Danish (English slides recommended)

## Speaker Benefits

- Free conference admission for you and one guest
- For special circumstances, contact speakers@bsidesaarhus.dk

## Submission Guidelines

- No sales pitches or vendor marketing
- Employer logos limited to title slide and biography slide only
- All speakers must comply with our Code of Conduct

Submit your proposal through Sessionize. Include a title, abstract, and brief bio.
