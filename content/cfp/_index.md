We're looking for speakers! BSides Aarhus 2026 welcomes talks on all aspects of information security — from offensive security and threat intelligence to governance, privacy, and career development.

Whether you're a seasoned speaker or presenting for the first time, we want to hear from you. BSides events are known for their inclusive, supportive atmosphere.

## What We're Looking For

- **Technical talks** — vulnerability research, tool development, red/blue team operations
- **Strategic talks** — threat landscape, risk management, compliance
- **Community talks** — career development, mentorship, diversity in security
- **Workshops** — hands-on learning sessions

## Talk Format

- Standard talks: **45 minutes** (including Q&A)
- All talks will be presented in one of two parallel tracks

## How to Submit

Submit your proposal through Sessionize. Include a title, abstract, and brief bio.
