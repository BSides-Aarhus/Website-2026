BSides Aarhus is a community-driven information security conference bringing together security professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts from across Denmark and beyond.

Founded as part of the global BSides movement, our conference provides a platform for sharing knowledge, networking, and building the security community in Aarhus.

Join us on **June 20, 2026** at **INCUBA Next, Katrinebjerg** for a full day of talks, networking, and learning — all at an accessible price point.

### What to Expect

- **Two parallel tracks** of hand-picked talks covering offensive security, defensive strategies, privacy, and emerging threats
- **Breakfast, lunch & refreshments** included with every ticket
- **Networking** with security professionals, students, and researchers from across the Nordics
- **Afterparty** to continue the conversations in a relaxed setting
- **Student-friendly** — free tickets available for university students in Aarhus
