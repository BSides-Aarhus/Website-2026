## Who We Are

BSides Aarhus is a community-driven cybersecurity conference held annually in Aarhus, Denmark. This website is operated by the BSides Aarhus organizing team.

**Contact:** info@bsidesaarhus.dk

## Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| cc_cookie | Stores your cookie consent preferences | 182 days |
| __cf_bm | Cloudflare bot management (security) | 30 minutes |

### Analytics Cookies (Require Your Consent)

We use **Google Analytics** to understand how visitors use our site, such as which pages are visited and how users navigate. These cookies are only set if you consent.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| _gid | Distinguishes unique users | 24 hours |

Google Analytics data is processed by Google LLC. [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Marketing Cookies (Require Your Consent)

We use the **LinkedIn Insight Tag** to measure the effectiveness of our LinkedIn campaigns. These cookies are only set if you consent.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| li_sugr | Browser identifier | 3 months |
| UserMatchHistory | Ad delivery synchronization | 30 days |

LinkedIn data is processed by LinkedIn Corporation. [LinkedIn's privacy policy](https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy).

## Third-Party Services

When you click links on our site, you may be redirected to external services with their own privacy policies:

- **Ticketbutler** (ticket purchases): [ticketbutler.io/privacy](https://ticketbutler.io/privacy)
- **Sessionize** (speaker submissions): [sessionize.com/privacy-policy](https://sessionize.com/privacy-policy)

These services operate independently. We do not control or have access to cookies set by these services on their own domains.

## How We Handle Your Data

### Ticket Purchases

Ticket purchases are processed through Ticketbutler. We receive your name and email for event logistics. This data is used solely for conference administration and is deleted within 6 months after the event.

### Speaker Submissions

Talk proposals are submitted through Sessionize. We access submission data (name, bio, talk details) for the CFP review process. Sessionize manages this data under their own privacy policy.

### No Direct Data Collection

This website does not have user accounts, forms, or other direct data collection mechanisms beyond analytics cookies (with your consent).

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing of your data
- **Object** to processing
- **Data portability**

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@bsidesaarhus.dk.

## Withdrawing Cookie Consent

You can change or withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the website footer. You can also clear cookies through your browser settings.
