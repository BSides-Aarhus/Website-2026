Ravshan is an Offensive Security Researcher at ONESEC in Tashkent, where he hunts new challenges and pushes the boundaries of cybersecurity. With more than three years of hands-on experience, his passion sits at binary exploitation, vulnerability research, and low-level security.

Beyond his research, he volunteers running cybersecurity workshops at universities including the British University in Tashkent and Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent. His talks include "Uncovering Memory Flaws with Taint Analysis" at KazHackStan 2025, "Unveiling eBPF: Reversing, Exploring the Verifier, and CVEs" at Positive Hack Days Cairo 2025, and Cyberkent 2025.
