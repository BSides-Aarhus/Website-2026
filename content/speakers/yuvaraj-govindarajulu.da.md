Yuvaraj is a technical leader with over a decade of experience in AI, cybersecurity, and embedded systems R&D. He is Head of Research and Innovation at AIShield, a Bosch startup with a mission to secure AI systems from development to deployment. With 22+ international patents filed, his focus areas include AI Red Teaming, GenAI Guardrails, and AI supply chain security.

He is a core author at the OWASP AI Exchange — a key contributor to the AI Security Testing section, GenAI Red Teaming Guide, and Agentic AI Red Teaming Guide — and an active member of standards bodies shaping global AI security, including ETSI and ISO. His previous talks span OWASP AppSec EU, BSI IT Security Congress, NeurIPS, DEFCON, and IEEE SecDev. He also served as technical lead for the EU Horizon Project "COBALT", focused on continuous AI certification.
