Behnaz is an AI Security Researcher and the Founder/Creator of the RAID-AI Framework. She is also Co-Author, Co-Lead, and Leader of AI Red Teaming at OWASP AI Exchange, where she actively contributes to advancing security practices for AI systems.

She has played a key role in OWASP initiatives, including contributing to the GenAI Red Teaming Guide for the OWASP Top 10 for Large Language Model Applications & Generative AI. She has spoken at Global AppSec Barcelona and OWASP Chapter Germany, and was invited as a panelist on AI Governance at the ISACA Conference 2025.

Leveraging the liaison relationship between OWASP AI Exchange and EU AI Act initiatives, Behnaz has contributed technical input to EU AI Act–related efforts, helping align AI security and red teaming practices with emerging regulatory requirements. With over 14 years of experience in computer engineering and network security, she brings deep expertise in secure AI software implementation, AI threat modeling, and adversarial AI resilience.
