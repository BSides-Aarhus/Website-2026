Bence is a Master's student at Aarhus University and a research assistant at Eötvös Loránd University, working at the intersection of cryptography, game theory, and consensus protocols.

His current research focuses on cryptographic accumulator schemes, economic censorship games, and quantum communication infrastructure. He has previously contributed to cryptographic modules for the EuroQCI quantum communication project and investigated trustless consensus attacks on blockchains and their implications.
