Bleon is a Senior Security Researcher at Exaforce, passionate about Infrastructure Penetration Testing and Security across Active Directory, Cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP, Digital Ocean) and hybrid infrastructures, as well as defense, detection, and threat hunting. He has presented Cloud Penetration Testing and Security topics at conferences including Black Hat USA, Europe and Sector, DEF CON, SANS Pentest Hackfest Hollywood and Amsterdam, and several BSides events in the US and Europe.

His research includes Nebula, a Cloud Penetration Testing Framework, alongside other tools and writing on his blog at blog.pepperclipp.com. He is also the author of YetiHunter, DetentionDodger, and Ransomwhen, and of the upcoming book "Deep Dive into Clouded Waters: An overview in Digital Ocean's Pentest and Security".
