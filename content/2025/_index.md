On Saturday, June 14, 2025, BSides Aarhus held its Kickoff Conference at EAAA in Aarhus — the first event after the association's relaunch. Two parallel tracks brought together ten speakers covering offensive security, malware analysis, cloud and OT security, mainframe hacking, threat hunting, and human-centred approaches to alert fatigue and security training.

This page is a permanent archive of the 2025 program, speakers, and agenda.
